Micro Invaders Competition

Micro Invaders is our current competition; a strategic robotics game where teams compete to develop the smartest AI, enabling two autonomous robots to perform manoeuvres in a fast-paced game of strategy.

About the Competition

Teams receive robot kits and must program their robots to navigate arenas, solve puzzles, and outsmart opponents. The competition combines programming skills with strategic thinking.

Micro Invaders 2024

Our latest competition Micro Invaders 2024 was hosted by HAMK Häme University of Applied Sciences and took place at Riihimäki, the future capital of robotics!

Frequently Asked Questions
What is the Robot Uprising hackathon?

Robot Uprising is Europe's largest hands-on robotics challenge where gaming, puzzles and storytelling are all combined. Robot Uprising has brought together over 500 techies and robotics enthusiasts to program, design and build robots to compete in our epic arenas. The Robot Uprising hackathon has been organized for three consecutive years in 2017 to 2019, after which we've focused on the Micro Invaders competition. We're currently rebooting our activities and planning future activities.

The story of Robot Uprising is set in 2042 and lore has an important role in our narrative. We offer an immersive alternative reality experience to our participants and partners.

Is this free for participants?

Yes, totally free!

Where & when is the hackathon organized?

Future events to be announced. Stay tuned! Micro Invaders 2024 includes a development period with workshops both online and at locations around the Helsinki capital area. The Final Tournament is hosted by HAMK and takes place at Riihimäki, the future capital of robotics!

Who is in charge of making the event happen?

Robot Uprising is a volunteer-led community that shares a common love for robots and making cool stuff happen. Our team members come from extremely multidisciplinary backgrounds from both students and alumni from the e.g. University of Helsinki, Aalto University and abroad. We are constantly looking for new team members to join our warm and ambitious team! We build everything with our own hands from robotics hardware and battle arenas to marketing and experience design. If you want to join us, check the About Us -page and fill our sign-up form!

Can I get credits?

For Micro Invaders 2024 study credits are available for students of HAMK.

Previously also both University of Helsinki and Aalto University have given study credits for participants of our hackathons. Other students can receive a certificate of participation that they are free to use for independent applying of student credits from their home universities.

What skills should I have?

The competitions and hackathons organized by Robot Uprising are intended for all robotics enthusiasts, whether your role is primarily in development, design, building or just scheming tricks to outsmart the opponents! When applying to compete, you should make sure that someone in your team has basic programming programming experience.

Do I need my own robot?

Robot Uprising provides participating teams the necessary robot kits for the Micro Invaders competition! See the competition page for more information!

About Robot Uprising

Robot Uprising is a volunteer-led community that shares a common love for robots and making cool stuff happen. We organize hands-on robotics events combining gaming, storytelling, and tech challenges.

Our team members come from extremely multidisciplinary backgrounds including students and alumni from the University of Helsinki, Aalto University, and abroad.

We build everything with our own hands from robotics hardware and battle arenas to marketing and experience design.
